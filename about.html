I am a theoretical physicist with a doctoral degree earned at the Friedrich-Schiller-Universität Jena.

I was born in Palermo in 1993 but I was raised in Parma, where I grew up and completed all my studies.

In 2012 I got my high school diploma in Parma at Liceo Scientifico G. Marconi.

In 2018 I got my master degree in Physics at Università di Parma with a thesis on the Gravitational Wave luminosity of binary neutron star mergers. This work was published in a high-impact factor peer-reviewed journal and can be found at https://arxiv.org/abs/1712.04267.

In 2023 I earned my Ph.D. degree in theoretical physics at Friedrich-Schiller-Universität Jena with a dissertation on Remnants of compact binary mergers and next-generation numerical relativity codes, which can be found published here.
